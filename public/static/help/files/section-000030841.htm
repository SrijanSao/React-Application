Verifying That Your Solutions Are Ready for Deployment

If your Solutions are not ready for deployment and you need to make changes, do so now. When your Solutions are configured for your environment, proceed to Initially Configuring the Stealth(core) Environment for Stealth(aware). (Configured Solutions include Profiles that have Group names and appropriate Stealth policies configured for each Profile and appropriate Stealth policies configured for each Channel.)

You will use the information in Initially Configuring the Stealth(core) Environment for Stealth(aware) to initially configure the Stealth(core) environment, and then you will return to Stealth(aware) to deploy your Solutions.
